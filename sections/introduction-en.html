1. Introduction back to ToC

The Managing Bridge Data Ontology (MBDO) is designed to represent, structure, and manage data related to bridges, including their components, materials, spatial characteristics, and construction attributes. It provides a semantic foundation for integrating and querying bridge-related information in a consistent and machine-readable way.

Purpose

This ontology aims to support infrastructure knowledge management by enabling data interoperability and integration across various systems that handle bridge design, inspection, and lifecycle data.

Scope

MBDO includes concepts for bridge structures, materials, spatial locations, construction details, and semantic relationships between bridge components and external datasets (e.g., geographic data, structural metadata).

Intended Audience

  • Researchers in civil engineering and semantic web
  • Infrastructure asset managers
  • Bridge inspectors and analysts
  • Developers of digital twin and BIM systems
