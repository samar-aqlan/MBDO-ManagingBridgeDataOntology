Bridgec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/brot#Bridge

An engineering structure which guides traffic routes (such as roads, railway lines, footpaths and cycle paths, waterways, taxiways for aircraft) or supply facilities (such as pipelines, cable routes, conveyor belts) on an elevated, suspended or free-floating structure over obstacles or over the surface of the terrain in such a way that there is a distance between the route and the surface of the terrain and thus often a possibility of undercrossing by another traffic or supply route is made possible. Bridges can be cantilevered between two abutments (single span bridge) or rest between them on several supports (multi span bridge). However, they can also be suspended from pylons by using ropes.
